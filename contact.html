Frequently Asked Questions

Do you take walk-ins?

Yes, we welcome walk-in guests based on availability. However, we highly recommend making a reservation, especially on weekends and for larger groups.

Is there parking available?

We offer complimentary valet parking on Friday and Saturday evenings. Street parking is also available, and there's a public parking garage one block away.

Do you host private events?

Absolutely! We have a private dining room that accommodates up to 40 guests. Contact our events team at events@tjunctiontavern.com for more information.
