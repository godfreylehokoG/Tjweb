Our Humble Beginnings

Where It All Started

In 2008, childhood friends Michael O'Connell and Sarah Chen transformed a forgotten corner building at the intersection of 5th and Main into what would become Cityville's most beloved gathering place. With Michael's background in craft brewing and Sarah's culinary expertise, they envisioned a space that honored traditional tavern culture while elevating the experience with modern gastropub cuisine.

The name "T Junction" pays homage to the building's unique location at a three-way intersection, but also symbolizes the coming together of different elements: food and drink, tradition and innovation, locals and visitors.

What started as a modest neighborhood pub with 12 beers on tap and a simple menu has grown into an award-winning establishment with a reputation for exceptional food, carefully curated drinks, and an atmosphere that feels like home.

Our Journey

2008

The Beginning

Michael and Sarah open T Junction Tavern in a renovated corner building with 12 beers on tap and a simple pub menu.

2011

First Expansion

We expand our kitchen and dining area, add 20 more beer taps, and introduce our first seasonal cocktail menu.

2014

Award Recognition

Cityville Times names us "Best Gastropub" and we receive our first "Excellence in Craft Beer" award.

2017

Patio Addition

We expand outdoors with a heated patio, increasing capacity by 40% and creating our popular outdoor bar area.

2020

Community Resilience

During challenging times, we pivot to create "Tavern at Home" meal kits and support local food banks with weekly donations.

2023

15th Anniversary

We celebrate 15 years of service with a renovated interior, expanded private event space, and launch of our house beer line.
