Brewmaster's Dinner

Date: Saturday, June 15, 2023

Time: 6:30 PM - 9:30 PM

Price: $85 per person (includes 5-course meal with beer pairings)

Location: Private Dining Room, T Junction Tavern

Event Details

Join us for an exclusive evening celebrating the art of craft brewing. This 5-course dinner pairs culinary creations from our executive chef with exceptional beers from three local breweries:

  • Cityville Brewing Co. - Known for their innovative IPAs
  • Crossroads Ales - Traditional English-style ales
  • Farmhouse Brewery - Seasonal and farm-to-glass creations

Each course will be introduced by the brewers themselves, offering insight into their process and philosophy. The menu includes both meat and vegetarian options (please indicate dietary restrictions when booking).

Limited to 30 guests only. Reservations must be made in advance.

Summer Solstice Party

Date: Friday, June 21, 2023

Time: 5:00 PM - 1:00 AM

Price: No cover charge until 9 PM, $10 after

Location: Main Tavern & Outdoor Patio

Event Details

Celebrate the longest day of the year with our annual Summer Solstice Party! We're extending our patio hours and bringing the festivities outdoors with:

  • Live music from three bands starting at 6 PM
  • Special "Solstice Sips" cocktail menu featuring seasonal ingredients
  • Outdoor grilling station with summer favorites
  • Fire pits for evening gatherings
  • Summer-themed decorations and photo opportunities

The party starts at 5 PM with happy hour pricing on all drinks until 7 PM. Live music schedule:

  • 6:00 PM - The Sunset Strings (acoustic)
  • 8:00 PM - The Longest Day Band (rock/pop covers)
  • 10:00 PM - DJ Summer Nights (dance mixes)

No reservations needed for groups under 6. For larger groups, please contact us in advance.

Acoustic Thursdays

Date: Every Thursday

Time: 8:00 PM - 11:00 PM

Price: No cover charge

Location: Main Tavern Area

Event Details

Wind down your week with intimate acoustic performances in our cozy tavern setting. Each week features a different local or touring artist showcasing their talent in a relaxed, unplugged format.

June Schedule:

  • June 6: Sarah Mitchell - Folk singer-songwriter
  • June 13: The River Bend Duo - Bluegrass and Americana
  • June 20: James Wilson - Acoustic covers of 90s classics
  • June 27: Elena Rodriguez - Jazz-infused acoustic soul

Enjoy our Thursday specials during the show:

  • $5 select craft beers
  • $7 house wines
  • 20% off shareable appetizers

No reservations needed. Seating is first-come, first-served. Arrive early for the best spots!
